**Built for people who ship client work for a living.**

## Four commitments. Each enforced by an architectural choice.

Zazz commits to four things: Professional, Precise, Polished, Portable. None of them are marketing language. Each one is backed by a decision in the kit that you can read in the code, the variables, or the Figma library.

## Professional

Zazz is for designers and developers who need a battle-tested design system that works across ever-changing design tools and development frameworks.

## Precise

Zazz is variables-driven, built to be flexible enough for most design requirements while ensuring consistency.

## Polished

Zazz is beautiful by default, including everything you need to present to stakeholders with confidence.

## Portable

Zazz is built on industry standards and translates easily to code. Variables map cleanly to your chosen development framework.
