---
title: Card
description: A composition pattern for product and content cards built from theme tokens and utilities.
---

A card is a composition, not a single primitive — compose the `.card__*` parts with theme
tokens (`--card`, `--border`, `--radius-lg`) and utility classes. Drop a `.badge`, `.text-*`
classes, and a figure inside.

## Basic

<Preview src="card/basic" />

Build cards from `--card` / `--card-foreground` on a `1px solid var(--border)` surface, with
`--radius-lg` corners and `--gap-md` padding. The figure uses `--radius-md` and a 3/2 aspect
ratio.
