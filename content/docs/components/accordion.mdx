---
title: Accordion
description: Collapsible sections built on native details and summary elements, with zero JavaScript.
---

The accordion is a `.accordion` wrapping native `<details>` and `<summary>` elements.
Open/close state, keyboard support, and the height transition (via `::details-content`) are
all native.

## Default

<Preview src="accordion/default" />

There are no attributes to configure — author the disclosure with standard `<details>`
markup. Multiple panels can be open at once by default; give a group of `<details>` the same
`name` to make them mutually exclusive.
